        • Shows deception, breaks from India

      • loves his camera, doesn't really look at the trip
      • careless, childish, doesn't have any authority, ignores mr kapas

      • Mr Das doesn't know it - Mrs Das feels guilty, tells it to Kapasi

        • Mr and Mrs Das' (real) children

        • Tourist clothing vs Mr Kapasi - Mr Kapasi's clothing represents his social status - he takes care to not have it crumpled
