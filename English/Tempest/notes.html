1 Background

1.1 Hierarchy

  • God is the creator: created the world from chaos
  • Everything hsa a specific place in the hierarchy
    • God, Angels, Humans, etc
    • The "Great chain of being"
  • Fire, Air, Water, Earth
  • Macocosm: Universe, Nature Sky
  • Microcosm: sub-hierarchy (King, Church, Princes, etc or within animals, birds)
    • Is a reflection of the Macrocosm
    • There is even one for the emotions (Choler, Tears, Blood, Phlegm)
      • If there is imbalance, sin will come
  • If order is not maintained, devils and demons can come in: sin
    • There is always someone above you you must obey
  • Link to the story:
    • Beginning, the boatswain commands
    • Prospero lost his place as king
