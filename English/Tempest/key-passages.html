1.4 437 → 472

  • Relationship between Ferdinand and Prospero

Complexity of relationships through the demonstration of power, manipulation and lack of feelings.

1.4.1 Demonstration of power

  • Use of imperative + exclamation marks
  • Speaks more than the other characters
    • Controls the dialog: orders silence, decides when people speak
  • Use of rhetorical questions to show that he doesn't expect an answer
  • "I charge thee": power over Ariel too

I'll set thee free for this

  • controls Ariel's freedom

1.4.2 Manipulation

  • His plan: lies and says Ferdinand is a traitor
  • Manipulating individual people: he talks individually to everyone + aside too (to let the public know of his plan)
    • He incites Miranda to rebel - but affirms his authority

Pity move thee father […] O beseech you father !

  • She is begging her father
