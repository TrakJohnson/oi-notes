    1.1 The 1970s crisis

    1970 crisis: Nixon stops convertibility between dollar and gold

    • Nixon: stagflation, industry going down
    • Because inflation goes up, people want to buy gold as the dollar is becoming worthless
    • End of the Bretton Woods system

    Production is done in the Asian Dragons

    Oil shock: 1973 & 1979

    1.2 Liberal Poliicies and the decline of international cooperation

    Creation of the G7: most industrialised countries, to combat the crisis

    • France keeps intervening
    • US and UK pull out of intervensionism (liberal theories: Chicago school) → supply-side economy
      • supply side: cutting taxes on corporations (trickle down)

      • Deregulation: removing government restrictions
