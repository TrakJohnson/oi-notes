    1.6.2 2nd Red Scare

    Alien Registration Act

    • Federal crime of thinking to overthrow the govt

    Causes:

    • Public concern about spread of communism
      • Europe, China, Korea, etc
    • Atomic bomb

    Trials helped convince the public that Red Scare was good

    • The Rosenberg's and the atomic bomb

    1938: HUAC (House of Un-American Activities Committee)

    • Created for Nazis and Communists
    • investigated, asked stuff, asked people to denounce / give them names, etc
      • Witch hunt
      • Scared that they would overthrow the govt

    1947: Executive Order creates Federal Employee Loyalty Program

    • Not "loyal" means suspected to be communist
    • 2k resigned and 200 were fired
    • Loyalty oath becomes the condition of employment

    Was not and had never been a member of the Communist party or any organization that advocated the overthrow of the government of the United States

    • "Hollywood 10" refused to go and testify in front of the govt
      • Became blacklisted: couldn't work in the industry anymore

    Ex: New deal officials were accused to be communists

    McCarthy:

    • Used intimidation and fear to impose himself and spread the hysteria
    • He went too far and started accusing everyone
    • Eisenhower thought he could ignore McCarthy so that he "fades on his own"
      • Senate just voted to censure him in 1954
