3 Poles & Flows of Globalization

  1. Globalization is driven by several poles
    1. Commanding poles
      1. Territories de la Triade
      2. Global cities (holding FTNs, multinational organizations, etc.)
      3. Regional blocs (UE, NAFTA, Mercosur, ASEAN)
    2. Strategic locations
      1. Maritime interfaces
      2. Detroits and canals
  2. These poles interact through complex flows
    1. Major flows: between the countries of the "Triade"
    2. Minor flows:
      1. Flows of money, foreign investment (US → South America + Asia, Europe + Asia → Africa)
      2. Flows of oil and raw materials (Middle East → Europe)
      3. Flows of manufactured products (South America → US + Europe, Asia → Europe + US)
      4. Flows of people
