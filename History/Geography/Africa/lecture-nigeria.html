2 Statistics

  • 64% poverty rate
  • 3.4 million living in HIV
  • HDI ranking 152
  • 47% youth employment
  • 31% of young children underweight
  • 2/3 of the population are in agriculture
    • Mostly the poor ones

3 History

  • Hundreds of tribal groups and languages
  • Since independence: 3 republics, 5 coups and a civil war
    • Role of army in government
  • Democratic government now
  • Terrorist groups in the north (Boko Haram)

4 Development

4.1 Positive factors

  • Natural resources (although it can change)
    • Vast oil and gas wealth, mining, fertile soil
  • Human resources
    • Many young people that will be part of the workforce
  • Population dividend - Urbanization, increased years of schooling; entrepreneurial skill
    • Middle class is 23%
  • Fragile democracy - first time a leader was ousted by the ballot in 2015

4.2 Evolution

  • HDI increased of 10% from 2005 to 2014
  • Life expectancy increased by 7 years from 1980 to today
  • GNI increases by 88%
  • Actually met the first MDG (half hunger and poverty)

4.3 Inequality

  • 2/3 of the population (agriculture) earning 1% of the income
  • 6th largest agricultural producer in the world
  • Keeps increasing with growth

4.4 Potential for takeoff

  • Rapid population growth
  • Increase in education
  • Foreign investment
  • Rising middle class
  • Increasing urbanization
  • Diversification of the economy
    • For now its 90% of the economic

4.4.1 Urbanization

  • Rural migration
  • Forced migration
  • China
  • Slum dwellings
    • Lagos: 2/3 of the people living in slums
  • Lack of infrastructure

4.5 Challenges

  • Still corruption
    • 1/3 of the revenues stolen before they reach the treasury
  • Infrastructure
    • Not very good
  • Economic recession and low growth
    • Not enough to sustain the population growth
