2 Demography

2.1 A Fast growing population

1 billion people:

  • Highest demographic growth
  • 2050: 2B people
  • Some countries are growing faster than others

Demographic Transition has not been achieved

2.2 A young population

  • 41% of the sub-saharan population is under 15 yrs
  • 31% in North Africa
  • Average Birth Rate:
    • Nigeria is 6 (!)

⇒ Education is a very important challenge:

  • 80% of the children go to primary school in Gabon, but only 30% in Niger

Africa's population pyramid has a wide base (large birth rate, young population)

  • Median age is 19.7
  • The demographic transition has not ended
  • The World median age is going up but Africa's didn't really go up

2.3 Low life expectancy

  • Average of 55 years old in Afirca vs 68 yrs worldwide
  • Sickness: Malaria, HIV, water linked diseases
  • In sub-saharan africa only 31% have a basic sanitary system
  • Conflicts: in Congo, one can buy an army for $10k

2.4 Consequences

A problem: a source of social tensions An opportunity: Larger work force which should boost the economy

⇒ having a lot of young adults is good for any country if the economy is thriving, but if jobs are short it leads to problems.

3 Urbanization in Sub Saharan Africa

3.1 What is it ?

The increasing proportion of the population living in urban areas within a country or area

  • People are coming to find jobs
  • The cities grow: urban sprawl
    • No money, no time ⇒ creation of slums and shanty towns
    • People get locked there

3.2 Rapid Growth

Rapid urbanization in Africa for the past 50 years (since independence)

3.3 Effects:

Keeping up with the infrastructure is very hard: water, sewers, transport, electricity, roads, etc

PRO: Better than the country side, access to infrastructure, education, health (ehhh somewhat ?) CON: Source to riots and conflicts, fosters poverty, slums

Slums:

  • Crime, sickness, scarce food, pollution, insecurity
  • Concentration of poverty
  • Keep the wheels of the city turning

Kibera (Nairobi), 1million people in just 4km2

  • Low life expecancy (35 vs 57 in rest of enya)
