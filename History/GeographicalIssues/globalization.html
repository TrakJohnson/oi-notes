2.1 Definition

Globalizatoin as a concept refers both to the compression of the world and the intensification of oconsciousness of the world as a whole.

Integration of Trade, Finance, People: connect freely, one global marketplace

  • Changing technology, international trade increases
  • Marketing: producing more, clothing changing every 6 weeks
    • Producing at cheap wages, consumption en masse
    • Disposable products, no more repairing

Compression of space: further, faster, cheaper

3 History

Starts with the growth of sea trade, then industrial revolution accelerates

  • After WW2 picks up very fast (affluent society)

NIDL: New International Division of Labor

  • Division of labor: specialization in the production, specific steps done by specific countries / companies / people
  • Spreads the production process
