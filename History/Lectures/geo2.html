4 Development

4.1 Positive factors for development

  • Resources encourages investment
  • Large infrastructure projects (rail, port, airport etc)
    • Financed by China
  • Good rivers for Hydro-electric powe
  • Population of working age is large
  • Urbanisation (happenning too fast though)

4.2 Rostow's Model

  • Stage 2 → 3: you need specialisation, infrastructure, foreign investment
  • You need a growing middle class so that you don't stay dependent on exports

4.3 The Leapfrog Model

  • Going straight to mobile phones and renewable energies
  • Sending money through smartphones: lowers transaction costs
  • Solar power falling in costs: advantageous for people off the grid

4.4 Challenges

  • Dependance on natural resources
  • Demographic transition / Population growth + Rapid urbanization
  • Inequality
  • Poor living conditions: low HDI, poor sanitation, etc

4.5 Obstacles

  • Lack of investment
  • Climate change adding desert
  • Economic Handicaps: weak industry, inadequte infrostructure
  • Armed conflicts: you need political stability
  • Health Issues:
    • Malaria, AIDS, etc

4.6 Positive factors

  • Education increased from 9% → 40%
  • Life expectancy increasing
  • Population dividend
  • Debt reduction (Debt forgiveness)
  • Improved infrastructure
  • More democracy
